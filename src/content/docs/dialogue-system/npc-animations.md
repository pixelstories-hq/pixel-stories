---
title: NPC Animations
description: Configure NPC idle, movement, and action-played animations.
---


NPC animations are now part of the broader player and NPC asset workflow. Start with [Player and NPCs](/project-assets/player-and-npcs/) for the main setup flow.

NPCs can use movement animations, idle animations, and one-shot animations that are played by actions during events or cutscenes.

Use movement and idle animations for normal map behavior. Use Play NPC/Player Animation when a scene needs a specific animation moment, such as reacting, turning, waving, or performing an action.
