---
title: Feature Request
description: Request a new PS Maker feature.
---


Have an idea for PS Maker? [Open a feature request](https://github.com/pixelstories-hq/ps-maker/issues/new/choose) and share what you want to build with it.

Helpful feature requests explain:

- What you are trying to make.
- What is hard or impossible right now.
- What you expected the editor to let you do.
- Any examples from your project, screenshots, or references that make the idea clearer.

The more concrete the request is, the easier it is to understand the problem and design the right feature.
